    Pack Like a Pro

    Make sure to seal those moving boxes tight with packing tape so nothing falls out. Ensure your valuables are protected with stretch wraps, bubble wrap and moving blankets. For large moving boxes or heavy moving boxes, use a hand truck or moving dolly to make it easier to transport big items from one place to the next. Once your moving boxes are on the truck, secure everything with moving straps so nothing shifts and gets damaged while being transported. The Home Depot Canada has all the moving supplies you need for a successful move.

    Once your moving checklist is complete, you print the recommended supplies list, and bring it with you to a Home Depot store for a quick and easy shopping experience. We also offer moving truck rentals and dolly rentals. Call or visit your local store for more information and to make a reservation.
